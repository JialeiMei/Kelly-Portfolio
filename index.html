Mobile Prototype & UI Design & Animation Design

A mobile app prototype is a preliminary visual mock-up that looks like a real app, and demonstrates an app’s fundamental design and function but does not contain working code.

Tickitin Mobile Application

This is an interactive mockup / high-fidelity digital prototype of a mobile app for Tickitin company. It is a platform that combines buying tickets and socializing.

I designed and implemented the production of the entire application. Even though the screens are just images,so you can’t type in fields, use the GPS or camera, etc. But it allows you to go further than a few images, into things like creating links between screens or making a small area of an image clickable, scrollable and viewable on your mobile device just like a real app.

* You can play with it in Flinto App.

The software/language I used
  • Photoshop
  • Flinto
